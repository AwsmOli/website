Self-run project doing market making and arbitrage on Bitpanda Pro with personal funds. Written in NodeJS with TypeScript, using InfluxDB to track the bot's performance over time and Firebase to configure it remotely without redeploying.

The same arbitrage-hunting itch later resurfaced in [Rich Eventually](/projects/rich-eventually), aimed at EVE Online's in-game economy instead.
