Started this project at Bitpanda, working with the backend lead and UX designer to write a centralized spot exchange from scratch, then hired and led the frontend team around it.

## What I built

- The exchange frontend from the ground up in VueJS and TypeScript
- Real-time order book, chart, and trade data rendered with Canvas and Pixi.js for performance under constant WSS updates
- A single codebase bundled into native iOS and Android apps via Capacitor.js, alongside the web app
- Offline/installable behavior via Service Workers and PWA tooling
- Internal tooling on Retool for support and operations

## Role

Lead Developer Frontend — also acted as people manager to a team of 4 developers, hired to grow the frontend team as the product scaled.
