I'm Oliver, a software engineer based in Vienna, Austria, and the person
behind blkPixel FlexCo.

Over the past decade I've mostly lived on the frontend — VueJS, React,
Angular, TypeScript — building things that need to feel fast and stay
reliable under real usage: a centralized crypto spot exchange at Bitpanda Pro
(web, iOS, and Android, from scratch), trading tools at Stillman Digital, and
a market-making and arbitrage bot I ran myself from 2021 to 2023. Along the
way I've also led and grown frontend teams, which I care about as much as
the code.

Outside of client work I build things with my hands as much as with a
keyboard, and the range is fairly wide: trading and arbitrage tools (a
trading operating system, an EVE Online market bot), embedded hardware and
PCB design (a laser tag system built with my kids, DIY sim racing gear I
actually race with), and small game projects in Godot. I like picking up a
new discipline — electronics, computer vision, game dev — the same way I
picked up frontend: by building something real with it.

This site is where those side projects, the write-ups behind them, and my
CV all live in one place. Check out what I'm currently building on the
[projects page](/projects).
